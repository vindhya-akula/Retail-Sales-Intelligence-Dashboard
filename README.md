# 📊 Retail Sales Intelligence Dashboard

An end-to-end Retail Sales Analysis project built using **Python**, **SQL**, and **Power BI** to analyze business performance, customer behavior, and product performance. The project includes data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), business insights, and an interactive three-page Power BI dashboard.

---

#  Project Overview

This project analyzes retail sales data to identify key business trends and provide actionable insights. The dashboard helps stakeholders monitor overall business performance, understand customer purchasing behavior, and evaluate product performance for better decision-making.

---

#  Business Objectives

- Analyze overall sales and profit performance.
- Identify top-performing products and customers.
- Understand customer purchasing behavior.
- Evaluate payment mode preferences.
- Monitor monthly sales trends.
- Compare sales and profit across product categories.
- Build an interactive Power BI dashboard for business users.

---

#  Dataset Description

The dataset contains retail transaction records, including:

- Order ID
- Order Date
- Customer Name
- Product Name
- Category
- Region
- Payment Mode
- Sales
- Profit
- Discount
- Rating
- Order Status

> **Note:** June 2026 contains partial data, which results in lower sales and profit values for that month.

#  Power BI Dashboard

The interactive dashboard consists of **three pages**.

## (1️) Executive Overview

Provides a high-level summary of business performance.

### Includes

- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Total Orders
- Average Rating
- Average Discount
- Profit Margin
- Monthly Sales Trend
- Sales by Category
- Profit by Category
- Payment Mode Usage
- Order Status Overview

---

## (2️) Customer Insights

Analyzes customer performance and purchasing behavior.

### Includes

- Top 10 Customers by Sales
- Top 10 Customers by Profit
- Sales vs Profit Scatter Plot
- Customers by Region
- Top Customers by Average Profit per Order

---

## (3️) Product Performance

Analyzes product sales and profitability.

### Includes

- Top 10 Products by Sales
- Top 10 Products by Profit
- Top 10 Most Ordered Products
- Sales vs Profit by Product
- Sales & Profit by Category
- Average Profit Margin by Category

# Key Business Insights

### Executive Overview

- Total Sales reached **₹48.75 Million**.
- Total Profit reached **₹8.08 Million**.
- Overall Profit Margin was **16.58%**.
- UPI was the most preferred payment mode.
- Electronics generated the highest sales.

### Customer Insights

- Top customers contributed significantly to overall revenue.
- Customer sales and profit showed a strong positive relationship.
- The South region had the highest customer base.

### Product Performance

- Electronics products generated the highest sales and profit.
- Beauty Product 12 recorded the highest order frequency.
- Product categories showed varying profit margins, highlighting opportunities to improve profitability.
